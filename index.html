Caring a bonsai

Environment


Bonsai trees are very sensitive towards the environment. The ideal temperature for bonsai ranges from 16℃ to 27℃. When kept in cold temperatures the life span decreases. It may also kill the tree.

Soil


It is important to use the proper type of soil for bonsai. Some soils are too dense which prevents the root fron penetrating the soil where as some soils are too loose which doesnot hold the water.

Water


The moisture level of soil should be checked before watering. The humidity of the environment should also be checked because if it is very humid the trees donot require much water.
